About

Shelli K. Spence is a LinkedIn strategist and personal branding consultant who specializes in helping executives over 50 build strategic visibility before they need it. With more than 25 years as a mortgage operations executive—including roles as Chief Credit Officer and Senior Vice President of Operations—Shelli understands the unique challenges facing senior professionals in today's market.

Based in Reno, Nevada, Shelli teaches mid-to-late career executives how to position themselves for opportunity through her proprietary frameworks: the Career Insurance System for executives navigating ageism and career transitions, and Inbound Leads Made Simple for entrepreneurs and consultants building client pipelines on LinkedIn.

Shelli's deep executive experience spans more than two decades in mortgage operations, credit risk management, and team leadership. Her career includes:

Current Work: LinkedIn Strategy & Personal Branding

In 2024, Shelli launched Shelli's Socials, a LinkedIn coaching practice focused on strategic positioning for executives and professionals who refuse to be invisible.

Her approach is grounded in real-world experience: she felt the ground shifting in her own career and started building visibility before she needed it. When layoffs hit her industry, her LinkedIn presence gave her options. Now she teaches others how to do the same.

Frameworks & Methodologies

Shelli developed the Optimize → Attract → Convert framework, a system that helps professionals transform their LinkedIn profiles into 24/7 career insurance. The framework focuses on three core pillars:

  • Optimize Your Profile – Strategic positioning that speaks directly to your ideal audience
  • Attract Your People – Visibility strategies that fit your schedule and style
  • Convert Your Opportunities – Turning profile views into real options (job offers, consulting work, speaking engagements, board seats)

She offers both the Career Insurance System for executives building strategic visibility and the Inbound Leads Made Simple system for entrepreneurs and consultants generating client pipelines.

Philosophy & Approach

Shelli's philosophy is direct: Your employer doesn't owe you security; you owe yourself that security.

She believes that the market gets more biased against senior professionals every year—higher salary, higher benefits, stereotyped as "slower" with new technology. But she also believes that strategic visibility, built before you need it, is the most powerful form of career insurance.

Her approach isn't about becoming an influencer or posting constantly. It's about building a LinkedIn presence that creates options: better job offers, consulting opportunities, speaking engagements, board positions, or simply the leverage to walk away from situations that no longer serve you.
