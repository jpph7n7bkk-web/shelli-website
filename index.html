The Why

Why I built Shelli’s Socials

When I first showed up on LinkedIn™, I was a C-suite mortgage executive watching my industry implode around me.

My beloved employer shut their doors. Mergers and acquisitions were happening left and right. There were no openings to be found, especially any of interest.

If I wanted to stay relevant, I had to adapt.

And adapt I did.

At first, I was frustrated with my progress on the platform. I saw people killing it while I felt like a failure.

Sound familiar?

But I kept showing up and over time figured it out: I learned the system, built a plan, and executed it.

Now, I geek out about LinkedIn™ so you don’t have to.

I help executives, solopreneurs, and sales pros master their brand through a clear, repeatable framework.

Each element of the framework works in sync toward one goal: attracting the kind of organic attention that turns into leads.

So why did I start Shelli’s Socials?

Because you have something to say.

I’ll help you learn how to say it, so you can start getting paid for it.
